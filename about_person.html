JAY BACANI

DIRECTOR, J BACANI PTY LTD

Driven. Innovative. Passionate.

Co-Founder and Director of Manor Real Estate, with over 8 years of real estate experience in the Hills district, Jay says he ‘cannot imagine working anywhere else’. Jay has consistently ranked no.1 in Baulkham Hills for most recommended, most sold properties and total sales value since 2016 (as per ratemyagent.com.au). Also known for selling properties others struggle with, Jay has carved his name among the most reputable agents in Australia– by a simple formula of setting record prices, providing exceptional service and creating long relationships. Jay has a degree in Business and Commerce majoring in marketing and a sub-major in property. This has given him in-depth insights on the best way to promote a vendor or landlord’s property in any market situation. If you are looking for someone to help you give the right recommendation with any of your real estate needs, don’t ever hesitate to call Jay Bacani.
