The Manor Story

OUR STORY

Co-founded in 2016 by Directors Jay Bacani and Igor Jugovic, Manor has rapidly grown into one of the most well-respected industry leaders. Establishing its name in Baulkham Hills, Manor quickly expanded to become dominating competitors in The Hills District and surrounds. Manor was built on the ideology that property is all about people and this philosophy remains the core of who we are today. Manor is an independently run boutique agency that is proud to offer a more contemporary, tailored approach to real estate, an experience that is about more than just exchanging property. Manor is fueled by a community of diverse, dynamic individuals bound together by a collective passion and goal to achieve the best outcome for each and every client. We drive and embrace change within the industry, constantly updating and developing our skills to adapt to the modern day demands and expectations. Rest assured that your biggest asset is in the safest of hands.
